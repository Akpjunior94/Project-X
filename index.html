About

Saniswiss is the leading manufacturer of safe hygiene solutions for healthcare professionals and patient safety.
Founded in 2006 in Geneva by a team of health professionals, supported by renowned microbiologists, Saniswiss evolves hand-in-hand with the highest stakeholders in global healthcare. Established as a true reference in Infection Prevention and sustainable hygiene globally, Saniswiss offers groundbreaking clean technologies for all hygiene sectors.
